AP Police SI Sub Inspector Qualification

The candidate needs to have a bachelor's degree from any recognized university in order to apply for this recruitment drive. Candidates belonging to the Reserved category(SC/ST) must have passed intermediate or any other examination recognized by the State Government. Candidates who have higher qualification can also apply for this Recruitment drive.

DISTANCE EDUCATION

Candidates who have obtained Degree through Open Universities/ Distance Education mode need to get recognition from the University Grants Commission/ AICTE/ Distance Education Council, as the case may be. Without recognition, the degree won't be considered. .

AP Police SI Age Limit

The candidate should have attained the age of 21 years and must not have attained the age of 25 years.

AP Police SI Age Relaxation

Candidates belonging to OBC/SC/ST Category will get age relaxation for up to 5 Years Candidates who have been working for the A.P. State Government (Employees of APTRANSCO, Discoms, AIrcENCO, APSRTC, Corporations, Municipalities, Local bodies, etc., will not be entitled to age relaxation.

Exam Procedure

* The AP Police SI Prelims exam will be qualifying in nature.
* The AP Police SI Prelims exam will consist of 2 different papers (Arithmetic and Test of Reasoning/ Mental Ability & General Studies).
* For every correct answer one mark will bes awarded for every correct answer
* Both papers will weigh 100 marks each.
* Both papers will be conducted for 3 hours.
